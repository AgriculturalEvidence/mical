Mission

Our mission is to bring impartial evidence from peer-reviewed literature on the outcomes of agricultural management practices and food system interventions to anyone that is curious. We do this in a way that is geospatially explicit, so you can see first hand the scientific data underlying the big ideas and claims in the media and elsewhere on food system transformations.

Agricultural Management

A focus on the science behind the big claims in farming.

Geospatial Interface

A dynamic interface which rescales the strength of the evidence for any location on the planet.

Harmonized Database

More than 144,000 paired observations of yields.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind Farm Geek? Farm Geek is led by Dr Zia Mehrabi and Dr Navin Ramankutty, two researchers who had the pleasure of working together for several years at the University of British Columbia. Both are data nerds who care deeply about the latest evidence on sustainable food systems. What is JAMES? JAMES is a Javascript-based Agricultural Mapping and Evidence Synthesis engine, it is the code and architecture that makes Farm Geek work. Is JAMES automated? While Farm Geek is a dynamic interface, JAMES is not yet fully automated -- in particular the database harmonization process. However, we are testing and working on automated methods for continuously searching and scraping agricultural evidence from scientific publications. Why does JAMES not yet know about XYZ? We synthesize data across key questions and big claims which affect farming outcomes. We will add more data as we can, and as we gather feedback from the community on the benefits of this service. If you have a suggestion for an intervention type or outcome we have not yet covered please let us know by using our contact us page. Are there plans to expand the outcomes beyond yields? Yes, we are working on ways to incorporate additional outcomes such as biodiversity, and other aspects of environmental and human health. How can I provide feedback on this website? We’d love to hear from you. If you have any suggestions on ways to improve Farm Geek please get in touch by using our contact us page. Can I collaborate on this project? If you are a researcher, farmer, or anyone else that has ideas for new data sets and features for this website, we’d love to hear from you, please get in touch by using our contact us form. Who funded this work? This work is a side project and is largely unfunded, but was supported in part by Navin Ramankutty’s Canada Research Chair position. If you are interested in partnering to fund new or existing features we’d love to hear from you!
